About Gold Link International

Established in 2022 and headquartered in Thailand, Gold Link International is a diverse organization specializing in the export of perishable items and hotel facilities, with a particular focus on the Maldives.

Our services prioritize quality and customer satisfaction, emphasizing honesty, integrity, and humility in all our products and services.
